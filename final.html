Multimedia Final Project Video

This is the final project from my MMP 100 Introduction to Multimedia course. It features my character, Vermi, a person who is into goth and rave subcultures. However, they don't really know much of the rave scene, they haven't even been to one. So, she ventures out alone to a underground rave being hosted behind a bridge somewhere in the Upper Bronx.
