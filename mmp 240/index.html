HATED BY EVERYONE, LOVED BY NONE

HATED BY EVERYONE is a powerviolence/emo-violence/crust punk band emerging from New York City with a politically charged lyrical essence, bound to capture the outcasts and empower their souls to feel seen. We’re a band forming as of March 1st, 2026, and planning to have our first show in late 2027.

Our band in its most honest form would be short-lived because we do not plan on making ourselves commercially available to the pain–staking industry as is, we plan to get our energy in, our message and hopes held high to the world out.
